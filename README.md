# Privacy Policy for Bogey Journal

Last updated: June 2026

## Introduction

Bogey Journal ("the App") is a golf journaling and performance tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

By using Bogey Journal, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you sign in using Sign in with Apple, we may receive:

- A unique Apple user identifier
- Your name (if you choose to share it)
- Your email address (if you choose to share it)

We do not receive your Apple password.

---

### Golf Data

The App stores information you choose to enter, including:

- Golf rounds
- Hole-by-hole scores
- Practice sessions
- Goals and achievements
- Friends and golf contacts
- Avatar customization preferences

---

### Analytics Information

We use PostHog analytics to understand how the App is used and improve the user experience.

Analytics may include:

- App opens
- Feature usage
- Error reports
- Device and operating system information

Analytics data is used in aggregate and is not sold to third parties.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and operate the App
- Synchronize your data across devices
- Restore your data when reinstalling the App
- Improve performance and reliability
- Understand feature usage and engagement
- Respond to support requests

---

## Data Storage

Your data is stored using Supabase cloud services.

Data is associated with your account and protected using access controls so that only you can access your information.

---

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share data with trusted service providers solely for operating the App, including:

- Apple (authentication)
- Supabase (data storage and synchronization)
- PostHog (analytics)

These providers process data according to their own privacy policies.

---

## Data Retention

Your data is retained while your account remains active or until you request deletion.

---

## Your Rights

You may:

- Access your data
- Request deletion of your data
- Stop using the App at any time

To request deletion, contact:

support@chesterlabs.io

---

## Children's Privacy

Bogey Journal is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

---

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact:

support@chesterlabs.io
